about Unacademy

Unacademy is an Indian online education technology company that provides educational content and services through its platform. It was founded in 2015 by Gaurav Munjal, Roman Saini, and Hemesh Singh. Initially started as a YouTube channel featuring educational videos, Unacademy has grown into a comprehensive learning platform offering live classes, recorded lectures, and interactive sessions across various subjects and competitive exams.
